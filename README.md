<div>
 <h2><picture>
  <source srcset="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f635_200d_1f4ab/512.webp" type="image/webp">
  <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f635_200d_1f4ab/512.gif" alt="😵" width="32" height="32">
</picture> Problem Statement :</h2></div>
<ul>
<li>Rainfall has been a major concern these days. Weather conditions have been changing for time being. Rainfall forecasting is important otherwise, it may lead to many disasters. Irregular heavy rainfall may lead to the destruction of crops, heavy floods that can cause harm to human life. It is important to exactly determine the rainfall for effective use of water resources, crop productivity, and pre-planning of water structures.</li>
  </ul>
<br>

<div>

</picture> Problem Solution :</h2></div>
<ul>
<li> The solution to the problem can be achieved in various approaches but one of the most reliable and effective approach is by using Machine learning methodologies, which is one of the applications of Artificial Intelligence, is being used to implement the proposed system. The irregular rainfall can be challenging for agricultural activities which depend mostly on rainfall. To overcome all these issues this rainfall prediction feature is developed in this project. Nowadays a lot of research and work is being implemented in the smart and modern agriculture domain. Exploratory Analysis is characterized by a rainfall database comprised of various attributes.</li>
  </ul>
